What is Whoot! Stamp?
Whoot! Stamp is a dining rewards app that connects various restaurants. Every time you dine in your favourite restaurant, bar or cafe, you gain a Whoot! Stamp that will lead to fantastic rewards. Whoot! Stamp is totally free, requiring no prepayment or hidden charges.

How much does it cost to use Whoot!?
Nothing. Whoot! is completely, 100% free. No membership or subscription fee. However, you may be charged if you use Whoot! on cellular data, according to the rules of your network operator. We recommend that you use Wi-Fi when needed.
