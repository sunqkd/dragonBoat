How to gain a Stamp
Show restaurant staff “My QR Code” After scanning by restaurant staff, a Stamp will be automatically added to Stamp page
How to use a Stamp
When you have 3 Stamps, you get a Special Reward by showing restaurant staff your Stamps
When you have 6 Stamps, you get a Premium Reward by showing restaurant staff your Stamps
When you have 9 Stamps, you get half off on a signature dish by showing restaurant staff your Stamps
Note
You gain 1 Stamp at most every 2 hours
For terms and conditions, please refer to Me → Settings → Terms and Conditions
Actual restaurants’ rules and regulations apply to gaining of Stamps
