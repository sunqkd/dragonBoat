How do I create a new account?
After successfully downloading Whoot!, you can register in the following ways:
1. Facebook registration: open Whoot!, go to the “Login” page and tap “Facebook Login”. Whoot! will then use your Facebook account information to create your account.
2. Apple registration: open Whoot! Stamp, go to the “Login” page and tap “Apple Login”. Whoot! Stamp will then use your Apple account information to create your account.
3. Phone number registration: open Whoot!, go to the “Login” page, and tap “New User Registration”. Select country code, enter your phone number and continue with the steps to complete registration.
4. Email registration: open Whoot!, go to the “Login” page, tap “New User Registration”, and switch to email registration. Enter your name, email address and password, and continue with the steps to complete registration.
How do I link/change my phone number?
Go to the "Me" page, tap "Settings", enter the "Account Security" page, select "Mobile Phone Number", and continue with the steps to link/change your phone number.
How do I change my password?
Go to the "Me" page, tap "Settings", enter the "Account Security" page, and select "Change Password". Enter your current password and new password, re-enter your new password, and continue with the steps to complete password change.
How do I edit my personal information?
Select “Me” and tap your profile picture to edit.
